How to Play Sharkdle

Welcome to Sharkdle! The goal of the game is to guess the shark. Use the feedback provided after each guess to narrow down the correct shark. Here's how to play:

  1. Enter the name of a shark in the input box.
  2. Submit your guess by pressing "Enter".
  3. After each guess, you'll receive feedback to help you refine your next guess:
    • Grey: The information is incorrect.
    • Green: The information is correct.
    • Red: The guessed shark is not the correct shark.
    • Blue: The year of discovery for the correct shark is earlier.
    • Orange: The year of discovery for the correct shark is later.
  4. Use the feedback to refine your guesses and identify the correct shark.
  5. You have 12 attempts to guess the shark. Good luck!

Have fun and dive into the world of Sharkdle!
